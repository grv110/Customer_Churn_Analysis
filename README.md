Customer Churn Analysis & Prediction
📌 Problem Statement

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges faced by telecom companies. Acquiring a new customer is significantly more expensive than retaining an existing one.

The objective of this project is to analyze customer behavior, identify the key factors driving churn, and build a machine learning model to predict customers who are likely to leave the company.

🎯 Business Objective
Reduce customer churn rate
Improve customer retention
Increase customer lifetime value
Identify high-risk customers before they leave
Support data-driven decision making

📈 Key Business Insights
Insight 1

Customers with Month-to-Month contracts show the highest churn rate.

Business Action

Offer discounted annual contracts to reduce churn.

Insight 2

Fiber Optic customers are more likely to churn than DSL customers.

Business Action

Improve service quality and customer support for Fiber Optic users.

Insight 3

Customers with longer tenure are significantly less likely to leave.

Business Action

Create loyalty and reward programs for new customers.

Insight 4

Contract Type and Internet Service are major churn drivers.

Business Action

Use these features to identify at-risk customers early.
